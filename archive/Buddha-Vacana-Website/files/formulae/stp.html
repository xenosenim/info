Satipaṭṭhāna
— Presence of Awareness —
[sati+upaṭṭhāna]

These are the formulae by which the Buddha defines in brief what the four satipaṭṭhānas are (≈33 occ.).

Kāyānupassanā | Vedanā | Citta | Dhamma



Bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhā·domanassaṃ;

A bhikkhu dwells observing the body in the body, ātāpī sampajāno, satimā, having given up abhijjhā·domanassa towards the world;
