Miscellanea

Phraseology

High society

High Society is slang for the top denomination of poker chip at any particular casino or card room.
Generally, a stack is considered to be 20 chips. If the top worth of the casino's high society
chip is $500, then this high society stack would be worth $10,000. Chip value does vary from one place to another.

Sitting the apple

Could be a figurative term used to describe someone playing in a very profitable or high-stakes game.
Apple is sometimes used figuratively to represent something excellent or the "best."
Phrases like "the apple of one's eye" or "the Big Apple" (referring to New York City) reflect this usage.

Making a run

A period of time where a player experiences consistent success, winning multiple hands or tournaments in a row.
