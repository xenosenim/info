# Buy-In & Stack Size

## Buy-In

[!
Initial 'Buy-In' = 20 - 200 of 'Big Blind'
!]

e.g.:

%[m|IF]
>> ( 'Blinds' = $0.25 / $0.50 )
%[m|THEN]
>> 'Buy-In' =
>> 20 x $0.25 = $10
>> 200 x $0.50 = $100
