## Game

# Player Styles

### Loose
[!
To play many hands
To play weak hands
!]

### Passive
[!
To call frequently
To raise rarely
!]

### Tight
[!
To play strong hands
To fold weak hands
!]

### Agressive
[!
To bet frequently
To raise frequently
To call rarely
!]

#### Guideline
IF Table - loose / aggressive THEN play tighter
If Table - tight / passive THEN play looser
