###### Game

# Player Styles

## Loose
[!
Play many hands
Play weak hands
!]

## Passive
[!
Call frequently
Raise rarely
!]

## Tight
[!
Play strong hands
Fold weak hands
!]

## Aggressive
[!
Bet frequently
Raise frequently
Call rarely
!]

## TAG
Tight Aggressive

## LAG
Loose Aggressive

### Guideline

%[m|IF]
>> Table - Loose / Aggressive
%[m|THEN]
>> Play Tighter


%[m|IF]
>> Table - Tight / Passive
%[m|THEN]
>> Play Looser
