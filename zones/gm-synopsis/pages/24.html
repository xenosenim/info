Unpaired Hands Combinations

C  = Total Combinations
A1 = Available Cards for Card 1
A2 = Available Cards for Card 2

C = A1 * A2

Paired Hands Combinations

C  = Total Combinations
A  = Available Cards

C = ( A * (A - 1) / 2 )
