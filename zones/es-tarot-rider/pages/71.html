Seven of Pentacles


A.E.W.

Upright

These are exceedingly contradictory; in the main, it is a card of money, business, barter; but one reading gives altercation, quarrel – and another innocence, ingenuity [stems from a mis-translation of the French ingénuité ("ingenuousness")], purgation.

Additionally
Improved position for a lady's future husband.

Recurrence
2 - News.
3 - Infirmity.
4 - Intrigue.

Reversed

Cause for anxiety regarding money which it may be proposed to lend.

Additionally
Impatience, apprehension, suspicion.

Recurrence
2 - Women of no repute.
3 - Joy.
4 - Quarrellers.

S.L.M.M.

Upright

Money, Finance, Treasure, Gain, Profit

Reversed

Disturbance, Worry, Anxiety, Melancholy.

Description

A young man, leaning on his staff, looks intently at seven pentacles attached to a clump of greenery on his right; one would say that these were his treasures and that his heart was there.
