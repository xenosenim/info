# Groups

### Early Position (EP)
[!
Includes: SB, BB, UTG, UTG+1
Starts left of 'Button'
First to act after hole cards are dealt
!]

### Middle Position (MP)
[!
Includes: UTG+2, UTG+3, LJ
Starts left of UTG+1
!]

### Late Position (LP)
[!
Includes: HJ, CO, BTN
Starts left of LJ
Has significant advantage
!]
